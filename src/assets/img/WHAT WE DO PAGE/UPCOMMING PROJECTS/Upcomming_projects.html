SNF Pattern For Water Efficiency.


About The social networking forum’s SNF pattern

It seems every village in Maharashtra has strived for the easy availability of water and the Gram Panchayats, Government agencies, Social Institutions have worked for water supply. You may come across storage tanks, distribution systems and quite a lot. Somehow, problems arose as some or the other scheme fails and the shortage of water increases yet again. In this scenario the forum goes out looking for villages facing acute shortage of water in spite of potential sources of water is located nearby. A team of Geologist, Architects, Engineers, and volunteers is dispatched for the survey. All the solutions are carefully thought over on the basis of the report presented by the team. Firstly, all the potential sources of water in and around the villages are considered and studied. Utilizing the resources, a few additions to cut through the water shortage. Their process ensures least of expenditure with a maximization of satisfaction. Further operations include construction of storage tanks, digging wells, pipelines and water purifications wherever needed. Thus the SNF pattern functions to eradicate water shortage from the remote and backward rural area. Social Networking Forum has solved the problems faced by 5 villages Gadhaipada, Torangan, Shevkhandi, Fanaspada, Khotrepada.
