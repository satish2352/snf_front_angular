Educational Facilities

A citizen of a country is a great asset to it, however, an educated citizen is the greatest asset to the nation. We believe that the progress comes in with every educated individual who can now make a lot of contributions towards the development of the society. Unfortunately though, The educational somehow never reaches the remote areas of the country where there are many who wish to be literate. This is mostly due to the lack of availability of resources and educational material that cannot be afforded or obtained with ease. Educational system of the rural India needs to be strengthened and to do so. our organization sends forth an appeal to our members and followers for donations in any form – especially – the stationery materials and books that can be distributed according to the requests of our beneficiaries and government schools in remote areas. Based on the donations received, E-learning kits were provided to villages like Gadhaipada, Peth, Khambale. With these ongoing programmes. we strive to reach more areas with a better hope and more to give !!.
